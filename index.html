Attendence Management

The Attendance Management System is a cloud-based application designed to automate and streamline time-tracking operations. It replaces traditional paper registers with an efficient digital portal. The system ensures data accuracy, eliminates manual entry errors, and provides real-time tracking for organizations.

Result Management

The Student Result Management System (SRMS) is a web-based portal designed to automate the process of managing, computing, and distributing student examination records. It replaces traditional paper-based methods to enhance data reliability and speed up operations.
